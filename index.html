Muddhapappu Avakaya!

Muddhapappu Avakaya (ముద్దపప్పు అవకాయ) is a classic Andhra delicacy that pairs perfectly with hot rice and ghee. It combines the flavors of simple dal (muddhapappu) with the tangy and spicy taste of avakaya (mango pickle). Here's how to prepare and enjoy it:

Ingredients:

Muddhapappu (Plain Dal):
  • Toor dal (kandi pappu) - 1 cup
  • Water - 2-3 cups
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
For Serving:
  • Avakaya (Andhra mango pickle) - 2-3 tbsp
  • Steamed rice - 2 cups
  • Ghee - 2-3 tsp

Preparation:

  1. Cook the Dal: Wash the toor dal thoroughly and soak for 10–15 minutes. In a pressure cooker, add the dal, water, and turmeric powder. Pressure cook for 3–4 whistles or until the dal becomes soft. Mash the dal and add salt.
  2. Prepare the Rice: Cook the rice until it’s soft and fluffy.
  3. Combine the Dish: Serve rice on a plate, add muddapappu on top, and drizzle with ghee. Place a spoonful of avakaya on the side.
  4. Enjoy: Mix rice, dal, and ghee. Take small portions and combine them with avakaya for a delicious meal.

Tips:

  • Homemade Avakaya: If possible, use freshly prepared avakaya for an authentic taste.
  • Adjust Spiciness: Add more or less pickle, depending on your preference.
  • Serve with Extras: Pair the meal with crispy papad or fryums for added texture.
